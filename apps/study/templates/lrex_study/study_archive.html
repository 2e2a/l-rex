You can archive a study by downloading and saving the archive ZIP-file containing all contents of your study as separate CSV-files:

01_results.csv:
Study results
02_settings.csv:
General study settings, questions, instructions
03_items.csv:
Items for all expriments of the study
04_lists.csv:
Item-lists for all expriments of the study
05_questionnaires.csv:
Study questionnaires
06_blocks.csv:
Questionnaire blocks (if used)

You can then use this archive file to restore this study or create a similar study.
Please, download the archive:

Generate and download archive

Important: Please make sure that the archive ZIP-file is valid and all CSV-files contain the expected content. When you proceed, all study data and results will be deleted from the server.
