Welcome to L-Rex, a free and open-source platform for creating and running rating experiments.

L-Rex is designed for linguistic experiments in which participants are asked to rate stimuli on a scale. You can quickly set up your experiment with L-Rex if...

  • ...your stimuli are texts or audio files.
  • ...the rating is binary or on an n-point scale (e.g., a scale from 1-5).
  • ...you want to distribute the stimuli using a Latin Square Design.
  • ...you want to present your stimuli in (pseudo-)randomized order.

My studies

Start creating experiments with L-Rex or edit your existing studies. A user account is required - you will be asked to log in or register.
