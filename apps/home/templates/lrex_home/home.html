Welcome to L-Rex, a platform for creating and running linguistic experiments.

  • Supported stimuli types: texts or audio files.
  • Participants choose one of several responses (e.g., binary or ordinal ratings).
  • Automated Latin-square distribution and randomization available.

L-Rex is free and open source. All functions are available without payment, advertisement, or user tracking.
