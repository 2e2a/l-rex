Invoices are only available for residents of Germany (for tax reasons). By default, 19% of the chosen amount will be added on top for taxes. If you want to pay exactly the chosen amount, tick the box Including 19% taxes. If you need the invoice to be more specific (e.g., because further details are required for reimbursement by your university), you can edit the invoice subject field.

After submitting the form, you will receive a confirmation e-mail. We will get back to you as soon as possible with an invoice, usually within a few days. If you do not receive an e-mail, please contact us under support@l-rex.de.
